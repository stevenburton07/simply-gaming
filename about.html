About Simply Gaming

Welcome to Simply Gaming - a website dedicated to video gaming news, discussions, predictions, theories, walk-throughs, and opinions from the perspective of busy professionals, fathers, and students who also just happen to be very passionate gamers whenever we can get the chance! We are often compared to IGN, though we just focus on what matters most. GAMING.

Here is what Richard Boil had to say about our walk-throughs:

"As a father, it is hard to find the time to solve the puzzles, or find the next thing to do in a game when you only get maybe an hour to yourself every day. Simply Gaming makes it easy to understand what the next objective is and how to accomplish it."

Simply Gaming is a family friendly website. We do not use vulgarity of any kind, and keep content clean for all ages. Nintendo may or may not be our favorite because they tend to be more family friendly than their other competitors. We hope you enjoy your experience with us!
